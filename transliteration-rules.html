Transliteration rules

When writing foreign words (for example, proper nouns), you must adopt their spelling to Nao according the official transliteration rules.

  1. Bulgarian - Български
  2. Cantonese — 粵語 (廣東話)
  3. Czech - Čeština
  4. Chinese (Mandarin) − 中文 (普通话)
  5. English
  6. Finnish − Suomi 
  7. French − Français
  8. German − Deutsch
  9. Italian − Italiano
  10. Japanese − 日本語
  11. Korean − 한국어
  12. Malay (Malaysian/Indonesian) − Bahasa Melayu/Indonesia
  13. Polish − Polski
  14. Russian − Русский язык
  15. Slovak − Slovenčina
  16. Spanish − Español
  17. Turkish − Türkçe
  18. Ukrainian − Українська мова
  19. Vietnamese − Tiếng Việt
