Nao textbook: Letters and sounds

Nao has 19 native letters (5 vowels and 14 consonants):
a i u e o
p t k b d g s f h c l m n j
and 4 additional letters, used only in loan words:
y
z v r
Capital letters are not used. All letters have standard international pronunciation, except:
c = English "sh"
y = "o" in English "connect"

Pronunciation rules

1. One letter one sound. No exceptions. Everything is pronounced exactly the way it is written, letter by letter.
2. The stress is semantic and most of the time consequentially happens to be on the last syllable.
3. The length or intonation of sounds are not predetermined but are used purely for emphasis.
